“Drivers License” is the debut single by American singer Olivia Rodrigo. It was released on January 8, 2021 by Geffen Records, as the lead single from her upcoming debut EP.

The song unpacks the “multifaceted” emotions Rodrigo endured after a heartbreak. It received widespread acclaim from music critics, who praised Rodrigo’s cathartic songwriting and emotional vocals, and the song’s stirring production.

“Drivers License” broke a string of records, including the Spotify record for the most single-day streams for a non-holiday song, and the biggest first-weerk for a song on Spotify and Amazon Music.
