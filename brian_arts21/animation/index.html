I really enjoyed this exercise! I think I found inspiration from finding this animation of a galaxy and a sun and I wanted to recreate an abstract interpretation of moving and dancing shapes. I just messed around with the gradients until I came up with something that I enjoyed. Everything feels very light and balanced here. I'm quite proud of how this turned out! CSS Animations are some of my favorite things to play around with.
